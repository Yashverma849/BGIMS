Four Disciplines

From the foundational BMS to the doctoral fellowship, our four programs are calibrated to different stages of a managerial life — but linked by a common conviction: that good education insists on the difficulty of the work.

I. Flagship Programme

MBA
Banking & Finance

2 YearsFull-TimeAICTECFA Aligned

The Banking & Finance MBA is the institute's flagship — designed for those who will lead the desks of tomorrow's investment banks, asset managers, treasuries, and central banks. The programme integrates rigorous quantitative finance with the practitioner's view of markets, regulation, and risk.

The curriculum is mapped to the CFA Institute's body of knowledge. Students sit for CFA Level I during the second year. The faculty includes serving partners from Edelweiss, JM Financial, and HDFC Asset Management.

Duration
2 Years
Eligibility
Bachelor's with 50%+ aggregate · MAH-CET / CAT / CMAT / GMAT
Intake
60 seats · cohort kept deliberately small

Specialisations
Investment Banking · Risk Management · Wealth Management · Banking Operations · FinTech

A glimpse of the curriculum

Semester I
Quantitative Foundations

Statistics, time-value of money, accounting, micro & macroeconomics, business communications.

Semester II
Financial Markets

Securities, fixed income, derivatives, banking systems, financial reporting & analysis.

Semester III
Risk & Strategy

Corporate finance, risk modelling, mergers & acquisitions, regulation, behavioural finance.

Semester IV
Capstone & Specialisation

Dissertation, summer internship synthesis, advanced specialisation electives, CFA preparation.

II. General Management

Master of
Management Studies

2 YearsMMSMumbai University

The MMS is the classical general management programme of the University of Mumbai — and at MM BGIMS, it is taught as it ought to be: with seriousness, with case rigour, and with faculty who insist that students argue every position they take.

Five specialisations are available from the third semester onward: Marketing, Finance, Operations, Human Resources, and Information Systems. Each track is anchored by a senior faculty member with a minimum of fifteen years of industry experience.

III. Undergraduate

BMS — Bachelor of Management Studies

3 YearsUGMumbai University

Designed for the determined eighteen-year-old. The BMS is a three-year undergraduate programme that builds business literacy from the ground up — quantitative, communicative, ethical.

Students complete a mandatory summer internship at the end of the second year, with placements arranged through the institute's corporate-relations cell at firms ranging from boutique merchant banks to consumer-goods majors.

Duration
3 Years
Eligibility
HSC (10+2) with 45%+ aggregate
Intake
120 seats per cohort
Tuition

IV. Doctoral Fellowship

Ph.D. in Management

ResearchAffiliatedLimited Cohort

Our doctoral programme is, by design, small. Each fellow is admitted only after a defended research proposal and an interview with the doctoral committee. Areas of guidance include strategic management, financial economics, marketing, organizational behaviour, and operations research.

Doctoral fellows are expected to publish in peer-reviewed journals during their candidature. The institute provides full library access, conference travel funding, and a teaching fellowship that includes a stipend and tutorial responsibilities.
