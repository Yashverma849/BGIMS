— Faculty

Scholars, practitioners, and the occasional contrarian.

Our classroom is a quiet rebellion against received wisdom. Every faculty member here has either built something, broken something, or written something that mattered — usually all three.

Director & Professor of Strategic Management

Author, columnist for the Financial Express, and a thirty-year veteran of management education. Dr. Hattangadi reads Mahabharat for strategy and The Economist for tactics — in that order.

  • Education Ph.D., Mumbai · MMS, JBIMS · BSc, Mumbai University
  • Research Behavioural strategy, organisational ethics, Indian management thought
  • Publications Three books, 80+ columns, Business Review India
  • Teaches Strategic Management · Business Ethics · Indian Knowledge Systems
