— Alumni Spotlight

From the second row of 2008 to the corner office at HDFC Asset Management.

Priya Deshmukh joined the institute in 2006 with a Class XII physics scholarship and an unfashionable interest in fixed-income securities. She graduated valedictorian of the 2008 cohort. Today she manages debt portfolios at HDFC AMC worth over fifteen-thousand crore rupees. Last winter, she returned to BGIMS to teach a five-week elective on yield-curve thinking. Forty seats; eighty-six applicants.

"I want every BGIMS graduate to know two things — the institute remembers you, and the institute is open to you. Your second visit is more important than your first." — Priya Deshmukh, Class of 2008, Senior Vice-President, HDFC Asset Management
— What we offer

Membership privileges.

All graduates of MM BGIMS are members for life. No subscription fee. No renewal required.

Lifetime library access

Borrow up to four titles at a time from a 24,000-volume collection, including the Mukherjee economics archive.

Mentor a current student

The 1-to-1 alumni mentorship programme pairs you with a final-year student for six guided meetings over the academic year.

Career re-engagement

The placement office continues to support alumni for life — referrals, advanced-stage coaching, executive education slots.

Regional chapters

Fourteen active chapters — Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Delhi, NYC, London, Singapore — with quarterly gatherings.
